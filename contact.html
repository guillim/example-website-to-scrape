Special orders

If you'd like us to track down a title for you, please send the title, author, ISBN (if you know it) and your name to hello@inkwellquill.shop. UK titles arrive in roughly eight working days; US and continental Europe titles take ten to fourteen. We'll write back with a confirmation and a price.

Press & trade

For press enquiries, review copies, or trade matters, please write to hello@inkwellquill.shop with “Press” in the subject line and we'll forward you to the right person.

Getting here

The shop is a short walk from Marston-by-the-Bridge railway station — turn left out of the station, follow the river path for four minutes, then take the second cobbled lane on your right. There is no parking outside, but the long-stay car park behind the old corn exchange is six minutes' walk away and rather cheap.
