Where Quiet Pages Find Their Readers

For half a century The Inkwell & Quill has been a small, slightly creaky shop full of carefully chosen books, strong coffee from the back counter, and a tabby cat named Margaret who has opinions about poetry.

Staff Picks

Every week our booksellers each pull one title from the shelves and write a short note about why it stuck with them. Cards are tucked inside the cover.

Friday Readings

Authors, poets, and the occasional botanist read aloud in the back room at 7pm on Fridays. Free tea. Limited seating. Earliest arrivals get the armchair by the window.

See upcoming events →

The Children's Nook

A small room with low shelves, soft cushions, and a rotating cast of picture-book heroes. Story-time every Saturday at 11am.

Visit the Nook →

Book Clubs

Six clubs meet in the shop each month — from speculative fiction to nature writing. Newcomers always welcome; coffee is on the house.

Special Orders

If we don't have it, we can almost certainly get it. We work with three regional distributors and a small network of antiquarian sellers.
