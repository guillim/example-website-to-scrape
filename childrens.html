Saturday Story-time

Every Saturday at 11am one of our booksellers reads aloud in the Nook. Free, no booking needed, parents very welcome to drift off to the fiction room and browse. Recent reads have included The Very Polite Owl, The Mapmakers of Marston Hollow, and (controversially) chapter one of The Twelfth Lighthouse — which proved a bit much for the toddlers.
