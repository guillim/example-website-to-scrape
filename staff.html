Behind the Counter

The three booksellers who keep the shop running. If you'd like a recommendation, drop us a line — we genuinely enjoy it.

MS

Marit Sølberg

Co-owner · Fiction & Translated Literature

Marit has been with the shop since 2002 and runs the Translated Fiction Book Club. She is, by general agreement, the person to ask if you can't quite remember the title of a novel but you know it had a long Norwegian boat in it.

Email: m.solberg@inkwellquill.shop

Direct line: +44 7700 900 142

LinkedIn: linkedin.com/in/marit-solberg-bookseller-demo

JK

Jaya Kapoor

Co-owner · Non-Fiction, History & Events

Jaya joined in 2006 after a decade as a researcher at the county archive. She programmes the readings calendar and writes most of the staff-pick cards in the non-fiction room. She brings her lurcher Bramble to work on Wednesdays.

Email: j.kapoor@inkwellquill.shop

Direct line: 07700 900 153

LinkedIn: linkedin.com/in/jaya-kapoor-bookseller-demo

TC

Theo Chen

Co-owner · Children's, Picture Books & the Nook

Theo runs Saturday story-time and curates the entire Children's Nook. They wrote (and illustrated) Margaret the Bookshop Cat, which has somehow now sold out three reprints. They will happily recommend a chapter book for a seven-year-old who “says they only like graphic novels.”

Email: t.chen@inkwellquill.shop

Direct line: 07700 900 167

LinkedIn: linkedin.com/in/theo-chen-books-demo

FN

Florence Naylor

Bookseller · Saturdays & Sunday afternoons

Florence is in her final year of a Literature MA and has been with us since the summer of last year. She is responsible for the small but ferocious poetry shelf and for a faintly suspicious quantity of post-it-note recommendations throughout the store.

For poetry queries, please use the general contact form.

EO

Elias Okafor

Bookseller & Stock · Thursdays & Fridays

Elias handles the deliveries, the stock-room, and the careful arithmetic of which boxes can fit where. He also runs the loft cookery section, where he has strong feelings about which paperbacks belong in a working kitchen.

For stock queries, please use the general contact form.

MQ

Margaret the Cat

Senior Editorial Consultant (Poetry)

Twelve years old, a tabby, sleeps on Mary Oliver. Approachable but mercurial. Does not respond to her name unless food is in the room. Has been here longer than three of the human staff.

Communicates by proximity, primarily.
